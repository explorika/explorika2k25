FAQs – NZ-Fest

Where will Explorika take place?
Explorika will be held on BITM campus. Exact venues for each event will be mentioned in the event details section.
Who can participate in the events?
All Students of Kishkinda-University and BITM who currently enrolled are eligible.
Is there any registration fee?
Most events are free. If any event requires a fee, it will be clearly stated in the registration Forms.
How do I register for events?
Navigate to the Events section or Register Button, U will find all the events and their respective register links.
How can I contact the coordinators?
Visit the Contact section to get in touch with individual event coordinators.
How can I contact the Site Admins?
Visit the footer section or Contact section to get in touch with Developers & site Admins.
Can I register on the spot?
On-spot registrations will be available for selected events only. We recommend registering in advance to secure your spot.
Do I need to carry a college ID for entry?
Yes, carrying your valid college ID is mandatory for all participants and attendees.
Can I register for multiple events?y?
Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as their timings don’t clash. Be sure to check the event schedule.
Is group registration allowed?
Yes, for group events, one person from the team can register on behalf of the group by providing all team members' details.
